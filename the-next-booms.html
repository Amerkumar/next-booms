The core thesis

Every major technology boom democratizes something previously scarce. Computers democratized calculation. The internet democratized information. Mobile democratized access. AI is democratizing intelligence. Each wave rides on the infrastructure the previous wave built, and the next boom solves the bottleneck the current boom creates.

The biggest wealth creation happens at convergence points, not within single booms. The iPhone wasn't a phone boom — it was mobile + internet + computing converging.
